Our Story

Natasha and Caleb met at school and, as part of a wider friend group, began dating in Year 12 (May 2021). They spent many afternoons going on long walks during COVID and getting to know each other. Over the years they’ve enjoyed visiting each other’s churches, going on family holidays, and supporting one another through university. They’ve built their relationship around faith, friendship, and a lot of shared laughter, and they’ve always supported each other’s dreams and studies. After four years of dating, they got engaged in October 2025. They’re grateful for the support of family and friends as they begin this next chapter, and they can’t wait to start married life together in November 2026.
