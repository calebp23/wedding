Invites

Can I bring a plus one?

We are unable to accommodate plus-ones and kindly ask that only those specifically named attend.

Can I bring my kids?

We politely request no children attend the ceremony or reception. Thank you in advance for understanding.

When should I RSVP by?

We kindly ask all guests to RSVP via our website by October 10th, 2026.

Transportation

Where are the venues?

Please see the ceremony and reception locations on our Weekend Itinerary page.

Is there parking?

Yes — both venues offer parking for guests.

How do we get between venues?

The ceremony and reception locations are approximately a 40‑minute drive apart. We encourage guests to organise transport ahead of time.

Is the reception venue accessible?

The canapés and dinner area is up a staircase on the left. An elevator is available to the right for guests who need it.

Is there accommodation?

Yarra Valley Lodge — where the welcome drinks and after‑party are hosted — has hotel rooms available for guests who wish to stay onsite.
